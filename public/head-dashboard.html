    How Ugandan primary grading is usually reported

    At PLE, UNEB gives each examined subject a grade from 1 (best) to 9. Those grades are added into an aggregate (best total 4, highest 36). Division I–IV (and U) describe overall performance from that total aggregate, not from one subject in isolation.

    This screen matches the class dashboard Comments tab for Primary: enter marks out of 100; the system fills grade and remark from the grading scale in Settings. Overall division uses all subject grades for this learner.

    Same as the class dashboard: enter marks scored (out of 100). Grade and remark follow your grading scale; initials follow your display name in Settings. Clear the field and save to remove a mark.

    On save, spelling and grammar are checked (internet), and close misspellings of learners’ names in this class group are fixed to match the register.
